Collaboration with Fondazione Levi

23 December 2025

We are pleased to announce a collaboration with the Fondazione Ugo e Olga Levi. In this special edition of the International Conference in Immersive and 3D Audio, the conference will take place in Venice, at the historic headquarters of the Foundation, Palazzo Giustinian Lolin, overlooking the Canal Grande.

Fondazione Ugo e Olga Levi, founded in Venice in 1962 and based in the historic Palazzo Giustinian Lolin on the Grand Canal, is an international centre for musical and musicological research. It preserves, studies, and promotes the tangible and intangible heritage of music through its specialised library, research projects, and cultural initiatives in Italy and abroad.

Alongside its scholarly activities, the Foundation promotes the dissemination of musical knowledge through a significant publishing programme devoted to academic publications, scholarly series, and musicological editions. Committed to the education of new generations, it engages primary and secondary school students, university students, doctoral candidates, and researchers through scholarships, internships, traineeships, and educational projects.
